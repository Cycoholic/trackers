Lindley Gravel Grind 2019 - Tracking Instructions

All competitors are required to have GPS tracking on at all times during the race. This can either be done with your own phone, or there are a limited number of dedicated tracking devices available for rental.

Tracking will be viewable at the following links:
https://track.massiveadventures.co.za/lindley_gravel_grind_2019
https://track.massiveadventures.co.za/lindley_gravel_grind_short_2019

Phone Tracking Setup Instructions

Please try have this setup before the race. If you have any issues getting this working, please contact Gary van der Merwe for assistance.

  • Email: garyvdm+trackrace@gmail.com
  • WhatsApp: +27793405832
  • Android Phones
    1. Install TrackRace Client app.
    2. Open app.
    3. Note the Device identifier - Email this to garyvdm+trackrace@gmail.com

    Note: Battery saver modes often disable the GPS on the phone, which means the app won't work. If you want to use your battery saver, please test that the app works with it before the race.

    Note: If you used the Traccar Client in the 2018 race, please uninstall it.

  • Apple Phones
    1. Install Traccar Client app.
    2. Open app.
    3. Set the following settings:
      • Server Url: http://trackrace.co.za:5055
      • Accuracy: High
    4. Note the Device identifier - Email this to garyvdm+trackrace@gmail.com

Testing

It is highly recommended that you test the tracking app on you phone on a long ride before the race.

  1. Start the service in the app.
  2. Check the tracker is working in a browser. (do this before you go on a ride)
    Go to https://trackrace.co.za/traccar/DEVICEID or enter your device id below:
  3. Go for a long ride, or just leave your phone on the window sill where it can get a GPS signal.
  4. Check that the tracker recorded you ride correctly.
  5. Stop the service in the app.

Use During Race

  1. Start the service in the app before the start.
  2. Stop the service in the app after you have finished.

Rental Tracking Devices

If you do not have an Android or Apple phone, or your phones battery is poor, a limited number of dedicated tracking devices are available for rental.The devices can be collected at the race briefing, and must be returned to Gary after the race, before you leave Lindley. Please email garyvdm+trackrace@gmail.com to book a device.

R50 Rental
R400 Deposit which will be refunded on return of the device.
